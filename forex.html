What is Forex trading

Forex trading is the buying and selling of currencies.There are various forms of forex trading such as;

  1. Crypto currencies

  2. Bitcoin

  3. Forex
  4. Stocks


Types of candlesticks in Forex trading

The Engaulfing bar candlestick pattern

This is formed when a bar fully engulfs another candle.


There are 2 types of the engulfing bar pattern;

  1. The bullish engulfing bar pattern

  2. The bearish engulfing bar pattern

The Doji candlestick pattern

When this candlestick forms it tells us that the market opens and closes at the same price which means there is equality and indecision between buyers and sellers.
